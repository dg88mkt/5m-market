Claim Process

To file a warranty claim, please follow these steps:

  1. Join our official Discord server.
  2. Open a "Warranty Ticket" in the support channel.
  3. Provide your Order ID and a screenshot of the issue.
  4. Our team will verify and issue a replacement within 12 hours.

What is NOT covered?

The warranty becomes void if:

  • You use 3rd party cheats or menus after purchase.
  • You get banned on specific RP servers (we only guarantee the GTA account).
  • You share the account login with other people.
